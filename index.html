+1.35°C

🔥 Climate Collapse

Atmospheric CO₂ sits at 421ppm | a level not seen in 3 million years. The warming already locked in will cause sea level rise, mass displacement, and extreme weather that makes today's disasters look moderate. The science leaves no room for optimism without immediate, drastic change.

4.7M ha/yr

🌳 Deforestation

Every year we destroy 4.7 million hectares of forest | ecosystems that took thousands of years to evolve. When these forests go, the carbon stored within them goes with them, accelerating the very crisis we cannot afford to worsen. And we keep going.

73% Lost

🦋 Biodiversity Collapse

The sixth mass extinction is not a future event. It is happening in real time. We have eliminated 73% of monitored vertebrate populations since 1970. The collapse of ecosystems does not stay contained to wildlife. When the food chain breaks, we break with it.

9M Dead/yr
